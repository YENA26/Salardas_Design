"Chronicles of the Paradox Wanderer"

In the year 2150, Dr. Amelia Grant invents the world's first functional time-travel device, known as the Temporal Nexus. However, upon its first test, something goes awry, and Amelia finds herself thrust into different eras without control. As she jumps through time, she realizes that her actions in the past ripple into the future, creating alternate timelines and paradoxes. Desperate to fix the damage, she becomes the Paradox Wanderer, attempting to mend the fabric of time while encountering versions of herself from divergent timelines. The story explores the consequences of tampering with time and the ethical dilemmas Amelia faces as she battles to restore the temporal order.
