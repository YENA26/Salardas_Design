REASONS WHY SHE WROTE THIS STORIES

Jinky Salarda's compelling stories are not just products of her imagination; they are a manifestation of her deeply rooted desire to transform the extraordinary landscapes of her daydreams into tangible, vibrant narratives. Driven by an insatiable passion for storytelling, Jinky breathes life into her delusional daydreams, turning the ephemeral visions of her mind into compelling tales that captivate readers. Her writing is an alchemical process, a means to give substance to the fantastical realms that dance through her thoughts. Writing becomes a bridge between her inner world and the external reality, allowing her to share the kaleidoscope of emotions, adventures, and characters that inhabit her dilusional daydreams. For Jinky, the act of writing is a transformative journey, a way to explore the depths of her creativity, and an invitation for readers to join her in the enchanting landscapes of her vivid imagination. In crafting these stories, she invites others to experience the magic that originates from the boundless realm of her delusional daydreams.
